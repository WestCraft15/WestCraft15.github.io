V2 All Keys Video Guide

This video shows the location of all 8 Keys in the April Fools 2023 build. If you need help on a specific Key, it can be found below.

Key 1

Super Jump into the ceiling above the Peppino Statue in the Entrance Hall.

Key 2

You can break through the wall by the door to Floor 2. Continue in a winding pattern to access the previously fake Key.

Key 3

Roll through the wall below Fun Farm.

Key 4

Go up where the Floor 3 Pizzaboy Clone used to be. Then crawl right until you can't anymore, and Ground Pound to find the 4th key.

Key 5

Break into the ceiling to the left of Peppibot Factory.

Key 6

Ground Pound the floor beneath You bring a light?

Key 7

Enter and exit the Green Room.

Key 8

Floats around randomly on the top of the tower.

Now leave the top of the tower and reenter for the finale.
