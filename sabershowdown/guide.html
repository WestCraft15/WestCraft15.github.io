Part 2: Stamina's Inner Workings

As you probably already know, stamina management is crucial to your survival. But how does it work?

  1. Stamina regenerates at a speed of 8/second up to a maximum of 100.

  2. ATK vs DEF

    All swings have a base damage of 4x your ATK. You block 1% of incoming damage per DEF. A Form IV user (12 ATK) hitting a form I user (70 DEF) would result in 12 x 4 x (1 - 0.7) = 14.4 damage.

  3. Damage Penalties

    Doing certain things while swinging will apply a penalty to your base damage.

    • Crouching: 33.3%
    • Running: 25%
    • Blocking (Stacks with crouching): 9%
